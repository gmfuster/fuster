DOUBLES

These are the doubles and you need to memorize them.
1 + 1 = 2 2 + 2 = 4 3 + 3 = 6 4 + 4 = 8 5 + 5 = 10
6 + 6 = 12 7 + 7 = 14 8 + 8 = 16 9 + 9 = 18 10 + 10 = 20


Knowing the doubles, you can use them in the near doubles. You have a near double when you add 1 or 2 to one of the numbers in the double. For 2+2=4, you can have the near double 2+3=5. That is the same as 2+2+1=5
